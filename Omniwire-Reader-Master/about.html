About Tapestry

Tapestry is a writer's best friend

Instead of looking for new writers, stories and books on social media, you can now navigate through over 100 literary publications at the click of a button. As someone who reads every day, I wanted to build a product that could do for writers what Flipboard or Pulse did for regular news. Now, finding the latest short stories from literary journals and online zines has never been easier.

Tapestry also provides the option to submit your beloved (or beguiled) manuscripts to dozens of publications who offer submission services online. Both novice and experienced writers will benefit from the list, which will be regularly updated with open calls for submission.

Tapestry is also an editor-in-chief's best friend

One important part about Tapestry is that we do not host any external articles on our website, meaning that all the traffic will be directed to your domain. Using our growing audience and a dedicated team of advisors and social media experts, we will plan our engagement strategies around getting the most amount of eyes on your writers, regardless of theit size. In short, Tapestry is dedicated to democratizing literary writing online.
