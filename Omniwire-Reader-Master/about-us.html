About Us

Omniwire is way more than just your average news reader.

By combining local sources from over 150+ locations in The United States and Canada, Omniwire lets you discover stories and trends before they become regional or national issues.

Omniwire is a news reader that lets you navigate the news from a geographical perspective. The difference between searching for news on conventional news readers and reading on Omniwire is the hyperlocal focus that our newsgathering takes. Instead of relying on a large tech company to bring news to you, we make sure that local news comes first and is told by people on the ground, not by national news bureaus hundreds of miles away.

As we grow our product to serve localities around the world, we will be increasing the precision and reliability of our feed system, and will pay special attention to driving attention towards local activism, small businesses, small business growth and more. We are super excited to launch Omniwire and will be looking forward to releasing mobile applications for all devices by the end of 2018.
